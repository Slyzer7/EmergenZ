Problem Analysis

We have seen how the last decade has been perhaps the worst in human history. Ever since the discovery of the virus, and the despairing fact that you and every person you know is afflicted by it, humanity has been facing perpetual darkness. The landscape of cities, small and big, have changed for the worse. Governments around the globe have been dismantled. And death can no longer be a peaceful transition. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) in Atlanta was at the forefront researching a cure shortly after the virus was discovered. The power failures also affected the CDC, and after weeks of running on generators, once they ran out of energy, the built-in security features triggered a "decontamination" protocol, destroying and sanitizing the building and everything inside. The data on the virus and the research that had gone into it was seemingly lost. This is where our company comes in. We here at Emergen-Z were able to recover the data that the scientists at the CDC worked tirelessly to acquire. Employing scientists of our own that we have been recruiting and educating over the years, we have been improving on the data left behind by the CDC. Emergen-Z is proud to announce that we have found light in this darkness, and we are beyond happy to announce that we have found a cure.

Our vision is to distribute the cure in two distinct ways:

- EMERGEN-Z KIT: We will be providing emergency kits that any individual can use in the event of direct contact with an infected. These kits will include an aggressive, fast-acting form of our cure to be used only in the event of a bite or exposure to fluids from a contaminated individual who has not been treated by Emergen-Z. The Emergen-Z Kit will fight the virus and protect the user for 48 hours before having to use another kit, or get to a facility for treatment. - EMERGEN-Z KIT: We will provide facilities worldwide, starting within the United States, where individuals can come in and be treated with a permanent cure.
