EmergenZTM

Summary

Our company is the only surviving medical facility in the united states. It is being made after the fall of the US government and the CDC attached to it. With the surviving people and resources left behind by what remained of the original CDC in Atlanta, Georgia, we decided to start this company in its image in an attempt to find a cure for the virus and distribute it to the surviving population of the united states and eventually the world. With the resources at hand, it took around a decade to eventually find a cure called "EMERGEN-C" which we now distribute within our EMERGEN-Z KIT, a kit designed for temporary relief of the viruses effects until the affected individual can reach our facility and receive the treatment to be permanently cured from the virus.
