Personnel

In order to fully develop and distribute EMERGEN-Z, we will have to increase our staff to meet the demand. Our current staff consists of a research director, three research scientists, and a business director. Mass producing our cure will require us to hire many additional people. We will need staff to assemble the kits and individuals to work at the treatment facility. For assembling the kits, we feel that an additional 10 people will be required. This number includes one supervisor to oversee the other workers and manage production. With this amount of employees, we should be able to quickly ramp up production allowing us to distribute approximately one thousand kits within the first month to the people who need them most in the local area. Over time, we project that our production capacity for kits may increase up to 20% with the same number of employees as we continue to become more knowledgeable and efficient. For staffing the treatment facility, we require an additional 20 employees. Their roles will cover the various services we want to provide such as caring for the infected, conducting research, and offering counseling to those affected. Our facility will be lead be a Senior Research Director with two Supervisors to manage the day to day activities. We will need to hire 10 nurses to care for the infected and an additional support staff to fill in various roles and take care of miscellaneous jobs around the facility. Finally, we will need two fully armed security guards in order to fight off the endless hordes of zombies and scavengers.
