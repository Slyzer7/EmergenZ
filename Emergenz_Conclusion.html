Conclusion

After the fall of the United States government and the CDC, our company was created to find a cure for the newly discovered virus. Our company, Emergen-Z, is capable of providing emergency kits for individual use to help fight and protect the user for 48 hours after contracting the virus. Due to the collapse of the CDC, our company will need to build a new facility along with hiring an estimated 42 employees. These employees range from security guards to protect the facility from zombies to research scientists that will continue to find ways to help people with the virus. Emergen-Z also offers the ability to transport the affected patients to their facilities, where treatment can be given and counseling to their loved ones will be offered. To start this company from the ground up is to be an estimated $11,278,500. This number includes all of the projected materials to build the facility, along with everything else that is needed to help patients that have been contracted with the virus.
