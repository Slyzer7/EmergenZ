Plan of Work / Scope

To start off, we plan to build our facility so we can start setting all of the equipment up and start manufacturing the EMERGEN-Z KITS. Once we have the manufacturing set up at a steady pace we will start transporting these kits to different areas to the country. As our delivery drivers make way around the country, they will be directed to find areas to build new facilities and to find survivors willing to help. We hope this way of travel will help us grow our company and save lives at an exponential rate. After all of the treatment sections of the facilities are set up, we can start using our ambulance like vehicles to transport those in need. Once patients leave our facility they will be given an EMERGEN-Z kit and offered a free ride to wherever they were living before. By the time we are well-adjusted to the day to day operations we should be starting construction on the new facilities. The number of facilities that we are planning to build is undecided but we will cover as much area as needed.
