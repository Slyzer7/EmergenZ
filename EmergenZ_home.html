Introduction

EmergenZ is the only national public health institute still mostly operational within the United States. While still under construction atop the ruins of the now defunct CDC headquarters, it serves as our current operational headquarters, located near Atlanta, Georgia. Our goal is to distribute a variety of medical supplies to the surviving populace in our ongoing attempts to combat the spread of the airborne virus and one day cure the surviving populace of it outright. At the forefront of our medical supplies is the EMERGEN-Z KIT, which contain a temporary cure that can provide any individual bitten by those that have succumbed to the virus a 48-hour relief period. After 48 hours, an individual can then use another kit or instead come to our facility to receive the permanent cure. We strive to educated to populace of how and where to obtain our cure in these dangerous times.

It should be noted that EmergenZ is not in anyway a combative institute. We do not provide those that use our services with any training or advice on how to combat hordes of the undead or how to avoid them, only what they should if they are infected and are at risk of dying. EmergenZ does not involve itself in attempts to resurrect the recently deceased in a more sentient, less unzombified states or the potential salvaging of an undead persons personality. We here at EmergenZ strongly advise anyone from attempting any of these previously mentioned experiments. Based on years of failed attempts at curing those that have already passed on, we have concluded that it is physically impossible to turn an undead person into a living sentient being. We understand how difficult it may be to let go of loved ones in these difficult times but advise that it is in your best interests that they experiments not be carried out at any point during the remainder of your life.
