EmergenZTM

Emergen-Z Kit:

Our Emergen-Z Kit will include an auto-injector with our cure inside. We will have multiple types of kits available, ranging from single doses up to ten doses. The Emergen-Z Kit will also include items typically found on First Aid Kits, such as: gloves, gauze, bandages, adhesive tape, antiseptic swab, dressings, and more. The Emergen-Z Kit auto-injector has an aggressive and fast-acting version of the cure. This is not a permanent cure. The Emergen-Z Kit will fight the virus for 48 hours, at which point the user must apply a new dose through the auto-injector, or receive treatment at one of our facilities. A map with all our facilities will be included inside every Emergen-Z Kit.

Emergen-Z Cure:

The permanent cure for the virus will be distributed at our facilities. The treatment in these facilities will be more involved, and as such would require a number of hours to be completed. We use state of the art equipment to deliver the cure, which will permanently nullify the virus within the host with very minimal, short-term side effects.

Treatment Facility:

Treating potentially hundreds of infected humans per month in a safe and secure environment will require that we build a new compound. There will be two buildings with a courtyard in between surrounded by 10-foot-high fences with razor wire. The main building will be approximately 3000ft2 and will be where the treatment of patients takes place. The second building will be where the staff lives and sleeps. Only about 2500ft2 will be necessary because most of the staff will either be working in the treatment facility or out collecting supplies. In case of a need for a quarantine, steel cages will be installed outside.
